Detailed Description

template<typename T>
class ca::lin::thomas< T >

Thomas method.

Used for tridiagonal systems of linear equations.

\[ \begin{bmatrix} b_1 & c_1 & 0 & \cdots & 0 \\ a_2 & b_2 & c_2 & \ddots & \vdots \\ 0 & a_3 & b_3 & \ddots & 0 \\ \vdots & \ddots & \ddots & \ddots & c_{n-1} \\ 0 & \cdots & 0 & a_n & b_n \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} x_1 \\ x_2 \\ x_3 \\ \vdots \\ x_n \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} d_1 \\ d_2 \\ d_3 \\ \vdots \\ d_n \end{bmatrix} \]

where

\[ a_1 = 0,\quad c_n = 0. \]
