Direct acces methods and operators

When accessing matrix elements with methods of this group, out of range indices will not be caught and no exceptions will be thrown. This may be useful if you know that your will not access any forbidden addresse in advance, so could save a little time of conditionals.

Out of range indices lead to undefined behaivour.
